Educational or Informational Page

Being kind and honest online is just as important as how we act in real life. Everything we post affects real people with real feelings. Practicing empathy, respect, and responsibility helps create a safer and more welcoming digital world.

Digital integrity means choosing what is right—even when no one is watching. Simple actions like speaking respectfully, thinking before posting, and using technology wisely can make a big difference. Every message, comment, and decision contributes to the kind of online space we build together.

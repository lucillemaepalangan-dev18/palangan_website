My Journey

Life is like walking around our town. Sometimes, it’s easy and fun, like when you’re with friends, and everything feels good. Other times, it’s hard—like when you’re trying to figure things out and it feels confusing. But I’m learning that both the easy times and the difficult moments help you grow.

Being a good person—having faith, doing what’s right, and being careful online—is like having a map. Faith means trusting that things will turn out well, even when you don’t know what comes next. Doing what’s right means being honest and kind, even when it’s difficult.

Being careful online is like being a good neighbor: making sure you’re not causing problems for others. Every action matters, and all of it helps you become better and stronger.
